AI-Powered

Clinical intelligence, built in

As a doctor writes clinical notes, our AI analyses the presentation and suggests relevant drugs and lab investigations — sourced securely through OpenRouter, never leaving your server.

✨
Instant suggestions
One click from the note modal. Drugs and tests suggested in under 3 seconds.
🔒
Server-side proxy
API keys never reach the browser. All AI calls go through your Django backend.
🩺
Doctor always decides
Suggestions are advisory only. One click to add to Rx or lab order — doctor confirms everything.
